Relevance depends on two equally important components: the algorithmic model and the network of available offers. While machine learning models receive significant industry attention, the quality and depth of advertiser networks are equally critical to delivering genuine customer value. Direct advertiser relationships, continuous onboarding of new partners, and rigorous quality standards distinguish premium networks from those relying on affiliate sourcing.
